Hohmann Transfer GUI

An interactive tool that turns the orbital-mechanics behind a two-burn Hohmann transfer into a point-and-click delta-V calculator.

Overview

A Hohmann transfer moves a spacecraft between two coplanar circular orbits along an elliptical path. Reaching the higher orbit takes two burns: one to enter the transfer ellipse, and a second at apoapsis to circularise. Without the second delta-V burn, the spacecraft stays on the transfer ellipse and coasts back to its starting point.

What I built

A MATLAB GUI that computes the angular momentum of each orbit and the orbital speeds the vehicle travels at, then derives the delta-V required for each burn to raise the vehicle from the inner to the outer orbit.

How it works

The user sets the initial and target orbits; the tool solves the transfer geometry and reports the per-burn and total delta-V interactively, making the manoeuvre easy to reason about visually.
