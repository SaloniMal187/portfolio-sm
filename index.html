Saloni Malviya | About Me

Currently pursuing my Master's at the University of Michigan, I am an engineer driven by the challenge of making complex systems move with precision. My foundation in developing mission-critical hardware at ISRO and DRDO taught me the value of precision modeling and structural integrity. Now, I am merging that hardware expertise with a deep focus on control systems to shape the next generation of aerospace innovation actively. My goal is to be an active participant in shaping the future of the aerospace industry. I am committed to equipping myself with the skills and knowledge needed to drive impactful change.
