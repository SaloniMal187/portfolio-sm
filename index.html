Saloni Malviya

MS Aerospace Engineering · University of Michigan

I design and analyze mechanical systems where failure isn't an option. At ISRO's Space Applications Centre, I built a topology-optimized robotic end-effector for space deployment — validated through modal, static, and dynamic analysis across ANSYS, CREO, and MSC ADAMS. At DRDO, I led CT-based failure analysis on a high-voltage defense module, improving system reliability by 25%. My graduate work spans vibration suppression, adaptive spacecraft control, and structural DAQ systems. I'm looking for full-time roles in mechanical design, CAE/simulation, or mechatronics where hardware constraints and analytical rigor both matter.
