Vibration Suppression for Urban Air Mobility (UAM) Aircraft Using Nonlinear Model Predictive Control (NMPC)

Nonlinear Model Predictive Control (NMPC) framework to explicitly manage system nonlinearities and state constraints. Developed a high-fidelity 6-DOF rigid-body model incorporating aerodynamic forces and gyroscopic rotor coupling. Leveraged a quaternion-based attitude representation (13 states) to eliminate gimbal lock singularities during dynamic flight phases. Proved NMPC superiority, achieving stabilization with 30% less high-frequency control content than LQR. Demonstrated a 54% reduction in peak elevator deflection (12° for NMPC vs. 26° for LQR), significantly lowering airframe mechanical stress.
